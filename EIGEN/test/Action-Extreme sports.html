Skateboarding

Skateboarding is a unique blend of sport, art, and rebellion. It’s a form of self-expression that thrives in urban spaces and skateparks alike. Rooted in creativity and driven by innovation, it continues to evolve globally. From backyard ramps to Olympic arenas, skateboarding has carved a space in both subculture and mainstream sport.

Red Bull plays a vital role in nurturing this culture by sponsoring top-tier athletes and organizing progressive events like Red Bull Skate Arcade and urban skate jams. These platforms not only highlight innovation in street and park skating but also give local communities a chance to shine. Through high-quality films, behind-the-scenes training clips, and athlete profiles, Red Bull helps push skateboarding’s reach further, making it accessible and inspiring to a wider audience.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding is about freedom, adrenaline, and defying gravity in snow-covered arenas. It blends technical skill with daring creativity across natural and sculpted terrain. Whether dropping into a halfpipe or flying off a big air jump, snowboarders constantly push boundaries. The sport has become a winter staple, mixing style, athleticism, and thrill.

Red Bull backs snowboarding through international competitions like Red Bull Recharged and athlete collaborations that elevate performance levels. The brand supports training projects, video series, and creative events that highlight not just the sport’s high-flying action but also the lifestyle behind it. Red Bull’s investment fuels progression, fosters youth talent, and brings cutting-edge snowboarding content to a global audience.

Surfing

Surfing is a sport deeply connected to rhythm, nature, and timing. It challenges riders to master waves and their own instincts in ever-changing conditions. What began as an ancient Polynesian tradition has evolved into a globally followed sport. Each ride offers a moment of harmony between human and ocean.

Red Bull's influence in surfing extends beyond the shoreline through athlete sponsorships, cinematic surf films, and events like Red Bull Foam Wreckers. Their efforts promote both up-and-coming and legendary surfers, helping them refine their craft and gain international visibility. Red Bull also explores the science of wave forecasting and board design, supporting a deeper understanding of what makes elite performance possible in such a fluid environment.

Wingsuit flying

Wingsuit flying and BASE jumping are among the most extreme and visually stunning sports on the planet. Athletes leap from cliffs, towers, and aircraft in special suits that let them glide at high speeds through the air, often within meters of rugged terrain. Red Bull is deeply embedded in the progression of these disciplines, backing elite flyers and creating spectacular visual content from some of the world’s most iconic locations.

Red Bull has been instrumental in legitimizing and professionalizing these sports, partnering with elite athletes to create record-breaking stunts and jaw-dropping content. They fund expeditions to remote locations and support the development of safer gear, making these extreme pursuits more sustainable and respected. Their media projects capture not just the thrill but also the rigorous planning and athleticism involved in every jump.
